SPEAK NOW PROJECT

Our Mission

Speak Now is a project inspired by love and determination. My name is Gabriel Alfonso Pérez Zazueta, and I started this initiative inspired by my mom’s idea and motivated by my sister, who has autism and doesn’t speak. Our goal is to help children with similar conditions communicate with their families and live fulfilling lives. Through the Speak Now app, we aim to bring the power of connection and understanding to every family in need.
