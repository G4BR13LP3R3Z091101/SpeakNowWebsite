SPEAK NOW

Who We Are

We are a group of 9th grade students who want to make the world a better place. We believe in the power of technology to create positive change, and our goal is to leave our mark in the world by helping those who need it most.

Why Are We Doing This?

My name is Gabriel Alfonso Pérez Zazueta, and I started this project inspired by my mom’s idea. I have a sister with autism who doesn’t speak, and my goal is to make it possible for all children like her to communicate with their families. By creating the Speak Now app, we aim to make life easier for these children and help them connect with the world around them.
